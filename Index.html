Brett Farve is a former Green Bay Packers quarterback in the NFL known as the "Gunslinger", Brett played for the Packers from 1992-2010 and is known as changing the culture in Green Bay. Brett is known for his crazy interceptions and crazy touchdowns which ammounted to his only Super Bowl win in 1997. In the end of his career, he left the Green Bay packers and went to there cross town rival, The Minnesota Vikings. This led to the ascension of Aaron Rodgers and the sudden demise of Brett Farve off the field and on.
